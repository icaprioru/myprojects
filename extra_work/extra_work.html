The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound,developed primarilyfor hunting hare (beagling). With agreat sense of smell and superior tracking instinct, the beagle is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.
